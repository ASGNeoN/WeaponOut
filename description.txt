A compilation of features built around weapons and accessories:

* Hide/Show weapons being held via 1st accessory slot visibilty.
* Temporary spawn point tents! Silk + Wood.
* Whips! Deals crits when hitting at the tip.
* Fists! Combo punch things, become INVINCIBLE.
* Dual-use Weapons! Right-click for secondary weapon abilities.
* More shields! 
* Endgame powerup accessories! Buff weapons to lunar tier.

And a bunch of other stuff!

MULTIPLAYER: Partially functional, but not reliably so.
